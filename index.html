Case 01 / Brand & Web

Cooseing.

Brand and website for Max Mancuso — HYROX PRO athlete, online coach, and faith-driven creator in Charleston, SC.

Client Max Mancuso Role Brand · Web Design · Build Live quinn-berry.github.io/Cooseing

Cooseing rests on a clear brand line — "Disciple First. Athlete Second." — and the site carries that voice across a single, scrolling page: mission, a results section with real race finishes, a coaching funnel into RevitalizeU, sponsors, a community run club, and contact. I shaped the brand voice and designed the experience around one outcome: turning an audience into coaching clients. Across the engagement, Max's social following grew from roughly 200 to 80,000+.

Personal Research · 2024–2026

LC15 & Trident-15 — decision design, in code.

Two self-directed projects exploring how design thinking applies to dense, time-pressured decisions. LC15 is a confidence-scoring engine, screener, and HTML dashboard (iterated v1–v4) for intraday equity setups. Trident-15 is a Python automation layer integrating a brokerage API (via ib_insync) and a market-data API, with a 25-field trade-journaling pipeline. Both paper-trading, both informing how I think about designing decision surfaces.
